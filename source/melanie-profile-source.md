# Melanie Montague

## About Me

Hi! I'm Melanie Montague, a student at Brigham Young University studying **Strategic Management**. I enjoy building meaningful relationships, serving others, and continually learning new things.

My life revolves around a few passions:

- Tennis is a huge part of my life. My office is often a tennis court, where I give lessons every day and help players improve their skills and confidence.
- I love tennis equipment from **Babolat** and **Wilson**.
- I'm currently a student at **Brigham Young University (BYU)** studying Strategic Management.
- I enjoy baking homemade bread, especially sourdough and other artisan-style loaves.
- Reading books is one of my favorite ways to relax and learn.
- I serve as a Sunday School teacher in my church congregation.
- I graduated from **Mesa High School** in Mesa, Arizona, in 2023.
- I served a mission for **The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints** in **Boston, Massachusetts**, where I learned valuable lessons about faith, service, and leadership.
